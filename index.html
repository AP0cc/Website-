My Favourite Photographs

These are a selection of some of my favourite
photos varying from sports to nature and even
general photos that have touched or moved me
in some way.

Sports

Sports photography has always been a passion of mine. Capturing the intensity, the motion, and the emotion in a single frame is what makes it special.

Kobe Bryant after scoring 81 points in the NBA—a truly remarkable performance.
Messi after scoring a brace in the first leg of the Champions League, only for Liverpool to score 4 goals.
Eliud Kipchoge broke his own marathon world record at the 2022 Berlin Marathon, finishing in 2:01:09. This performance further cemented his legacy as one of the greatest marathon runners ever.

Nature

Nature photography allows us to capture the beauty of the world around us, from breathtaking landscapes to the smallest details in flora and fauna.

An aurora is a mesmerizing light display in the night sky, caused by the interaction of solar winds with Earth's magnetic field, often seen near the polar regions.
Bioluminescence: The subtle light of the ocean, where living organisms create a soft, natural glow in the dark.
Volcanic eruptions reveal Earth’s fiery power, altering landscapes with bursts of lava and ash.

Humans

Human photography is about capturing the essence of a moment in someone's life - the raw emotion, the story, the depth of humanity.

A candid shot of a street performer.
Martin Luther King Jr fighting for African American rights in the United States.
Antony, renowned as one of football’s all-time greats, is celebrated for his extraordinary skill, versatility, and significant contributions to the sport.
